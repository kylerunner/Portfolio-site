A Better Way to Share Cash With friends

Problem Statement

Sharing money with friends and family can be awkward or inconvenient. Rhino Splits aims to solve this by creating a more natural and trusted solution to share and request money with those you trust most - all while also empowering each user to give back.

The Approach

I started by evaluating the most relevant competitors in this space. After getting a feel for what is available for the average user of a cash app, I began my interview process among the target market for this application - Millennials.


A common theme that came up in the interviews was that for the apps that many of them use - the social integrations are fun but meaningless. One particular interview brought up the concept of being able to support causes within a money app - which is who my persona is based upon.


The idea of Rhino Splits is to not only improve the cash app so friends can better share money - but also to give millennials an integrated and encouraging way to make a difference where they can - by paying it forward.
